About Us

Bangalore is the main and prominent location for apparel manufacturing with new brands popping up everyday in the market. The garments industry is growing and changing at a pace faster than ever before. If a brand is given the right direction with proper quality of products then it can help them leverage the market opportunity better.


Our garments setup is an age old one with more than 20 years of existence in the industry with development and production of high quality garments has been at its all time high which not only helps many brands with quality products but also gives livelihood to many households by creating a lot of employment opportunities.

Our garment production is an organized activity consisting of sequential processes such as laying ,marking ,cutting, stitching, checking, finishing, pressing and packaging. Whether it is a Ready-to-wear apparel or garment manufacturing for a brand, it involves many steps that are to be followed , beginning with the idea or design concept and ending with a final finished product. Every order or product being manufactured involves and goes through a series of steps which include design, fabric selection and inspection, pattern making, grading, marking, spreading, cutting, bundling, sewing, pressing, finishing and detailing ,dyeing and washing. Utmost care is taken and monitored at each stage to avoid defects and make corrections, if any.
