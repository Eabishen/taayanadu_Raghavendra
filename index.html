About Us

Taaynadu Raghvendra is an Indian citizen who hails from the southern state of India - Karnataka. His native roots go back to Sorahra Taluk, Chandragutti Hobli, Badada Bailu Village, in Shimoga district of Karnataka. Speaking of his personal life, his family comprises Smt Kusumamma and Late Veerabhadrappa Gowda and his 3 siblings. He is the last and youngest one among the siblings and their respective names are Gangamma, Sadananda and Manjunath respectively.

Mr. Raghavendra has been an average student at school whose only focus was to do more of things which involve practical doings and not just theoretical learning. He started his journey of being independent by being self-employed with a very strong determination to achieve something great in life. He has gone through a lot of hardships & struggles in life but he has moved forward positively with the sole intention of serving his nation and his state on the whole and giving back to his home country.

He has initiated and established various bodies including the Karnataka Rakshana Vedike Gajaseve Sangha for the empowering and preserving of local culture and regional language. His sole direction is to serve and protect age-old practices of our ancestors and do so for the welfare of the people at large with the support of many gurus, political leaders and other elderly people. Mr. Raghavendra believes that all people are one and doesn't do any discrimination on the basis of caste, religion, rich & poor and treats everyone in a friendly and equal manner.
