About Us

Ambulance Seva App is an platform for availing of ambulance services for various needs relating to healthcare. During times of an emergency, timely arrival of ambulance and healthcare is of utmost importance and very crucial to ones life, considering the urgency of the situation and also the very unstructured functioning of this space in the healthcare segment, we have launched this application with clear and transparent prices for the services to be known by the people prior to booking according to the time and distance required.


Our ambulance network helps you connect to the nearby immediately available source of vehicle in no time helping you get the required assistance at the right time of need. We have all ranges of vehicles that are available starting from basic small vans to big vehicles with all loaded facilities. All ambulances depending on the requirement come with specially trained staff and medical technicians which help in guiding and better monitoring of the situation in hand.
